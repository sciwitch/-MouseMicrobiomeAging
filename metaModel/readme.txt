metabolicModelMicrobiome
------------------------
Contains the metabolic models reconstructed from MAGs of the mouse microbiota.
The required metabolic models have been seperately uploaded to a zenodo record (doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.10844503)
and can be obtained from https://zenodo.org/records/10844503/files/aging_cohort_microbiota_SBML.7z?download=1


metamodel
---------
Contains scripts and data relating to the reconstruction and analysis of the metabolic metamodel.
